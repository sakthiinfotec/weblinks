#### Terraform

##### Advantages
* Multi-cloud
* Stateful
* Version controlled
* Declarative
* Scalable (Eg. count - To Spin off multiple instances)
* Saves Cost (Spin off the entire infrastructure when needed and Tear down when not in needed)
* Disaster Recovery
* Reduces Human Error

##### Lifecycle
* init
* validate
* plan
* apply
* destroy

* Main
