## NYC Taxi Data
Datasets of trip records for various New York City (NYC) transportation services, including Yellow Taxis, Green Taxis, and For-Hire Vehicles (like Uber and Lyft), all in the efficient Parquet file format

- [TLC Trip Record Data - NYC.gov](https://www.nyc.gov/site/tlc/about/tlc-trip-record-data.page]
- [The TLC trip record data repository on Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/salikhussaini49/nyc-tlc-trip-record-data-repository)
- [Azure (Microsoft Learn)](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/open-datasets/dataset-taxi-yellow)
- [AWS (Registry of Open Data on AWS)](https://registry.opendata.aws/nyc-tlc-trip-records-pds/)
- [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/abuchionwuegbusi/green-taxi-trip-data)
- [Data.gov](https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/?_tags_limit=0&publisher=data.cityofnewyork.us&organization=city-of-new-york&tags=trip)
